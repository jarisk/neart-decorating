Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function correctly and provide information to the site owner about how the site is being used.

2. How We Use Cookies

The Neart Decorating website is a simple static site. We keep cookie use to an absolute minimum. We do not use advertising, tracking, or profiling cookies.

3. Cookies on Our Site

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
__cf_bm Cloudflare Bot management and security — distinguishes between humans and automated traffic 30 minutes
_cfuvid Cloudflare Rate limiting — helps manage traffic to protect the site Session

These cookies are set by Cloudflare, which hosts and protects our website. They are strictly necessary for the site to operate securely and cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Fonts to display text. Google Fonts loads fonts from Google's servers and may set its own cookies or log your IP address in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Our contact form uses Basin to process submissions. Basin may set session cookies when the form is submitted. See Basin's Privacy Policy for details.

5. How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some websites. For more information on managing cookies, visit:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if we add new features to our website. The latest version will always be available on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at info@neartdecorating.co.uk.
